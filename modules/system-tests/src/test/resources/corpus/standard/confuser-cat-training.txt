Cat Training Basics for Pet Owners

While cats are often considered independent and untrainable, they can actually
learn various behaviors through positive reinforcement techniques.

Trainable Behaviors:
- Using the litter box (usually instinctive)
- Coming when called
- Sitting on command
- High-five or shake paws
- Walking on a leash

Training Principles:
- Use treats and positive reinforcement
- Keep sessions short (5-10 minutes)
- Be patient - cats learn at their own pace
- Never use punishment
- Consistency is key

Common Challenges:
- Scratching furniture (provide alternatives)
- Jumping on counters (use deterrent sprays)
- Nocturnal activity (schedule play before bed)
