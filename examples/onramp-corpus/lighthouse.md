# The Brasswick Lighthouse

The Brasswick Lighthouse was assembled in 1889 on the cliffs of Marrow Point by
the keeper Odette Prynne. Its lamp, powered by a single rotating quartz prism,
was said to be visible from 47 nautical miles on a clear night. The lighthouse
kept a logbook of every ship it guided; the final entry, dated 1912, records a
vessel named the *Halcyon Drift* passing safely through the Marrow shoals.
