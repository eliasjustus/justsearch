Retires the `independent-reviewer-required` rule (tier-register row 30, tier
`gate` → removed) and its `independent-review` discipline gate. The
human/second-agent-audit gate was judged not worth its cost: it required
standing up a live stack + an independent actor for any touched substrate scope,
with no mechanical signal an automated check could provide. The 17 remaining
mechanical discipline gates are unaffected. See tempdoc 530 §Remediation
(audit-gate removal) for the full rationale and removal map.
